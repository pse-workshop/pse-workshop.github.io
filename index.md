# Perspectives on Scientific Error 2027

**9th Workshop on Perspectives on Scientific Error (PSE9)**

*Date and location: to be announced*

---

The *Perspectives on Scientific Error* workshop series brings together researchers from statistics, philosophy of science, and meta-science to examine how false claims proliferate in research literature, why replication failures occur, and what can be done to reduce scientific error and restore public trust in expertise.

PSE9 will be the ninth edition of this workshop, which has been held annually since 2017 across institutions in Europe and beyond.

## Topics

The workshop addresses statistical, philosophical, and meta-scientific issues of scientific error, including but not limited to:

- Replication failures and their causes
- Statistical misconceptions and misuse
- Publication bias and selective reporting
- Open science practices and reform
- Philosophy of scientific methodology
- Meta-science and research on research
